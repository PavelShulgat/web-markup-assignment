Haarlem Oude Stad, is the vibrant and historic heart of the Dutch city of Haarlem. Situated in the North Holland province, just west of Amsterdam, Haarlem Center is a captivating destination with a wealth of attractions and a lively atmosphere. Haarlem has population of about 162.000. It located in 15-20 minutes by train away from Amsterdam and 30 minutes to Zandvoort beach. Oude Stad has population of 14080 and area of 1490 km2.

The center of Haarlem is characterized by its well-preserved historic architecture. Wandering through the narrow, cobblestone streets, you'll encounter charming old buildings. The architecture here is a testament to the city's rich history.

The city center offers an excellent shopping experience, with a mix of boutiques, antique shops, and markets. Haarlem is also celebrated for its diverse dining scene, encompassing traditional Dutch cuisine and a range of international options.Haarlem Center is easily accessible by train from Amsterdam, making it a popular destination for day trips from the Dutch capital. Its central location in North Holland also makes it a convenient starting point for exploring the wider region.

Haarlem Center boasts an array of cultural attractions which you can see here.

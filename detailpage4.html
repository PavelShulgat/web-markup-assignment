Advantages of living in the center: The city center is steeped in history, Haarlem Center is home to numerous cultural attractions, including museums, galleries, and theaters, providing opportunities for cultural enrichment and leisure activities. The city center has a tight-knit and diverse community of residents, contributing to a friendly and neighborly atmosphere. Living there means easy access to shops, restaurants, and public transportation, making daily life more convenient. Haarlem is just a short train ride away from Amsterdam, offering the advantages of a larger city while providing a quieter and more relaxed living environment.

Haarlem Center is a desirable location, and as such, housing costs can be relatively high. Rent or property prices may be more expensive than in outlying areas. The historic buildings in the city center often come with limited space. This can be a downside for those who prefer larger living spaces. The city center can be busy with tourists, particularly during the peak season, which might affect the tranquility of daily life and access to some places also can be problems with parking. Some streets may be pedestrian-only or have limited vehicle access, which can affect mobility for residents who rely on cars.
